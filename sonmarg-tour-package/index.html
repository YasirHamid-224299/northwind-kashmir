FAQs

Popular questions about Sonmarg travel.

How do I reach Sonmarg?

Sonmarg is approximately 80 kilometers from Srinagar and is reached by private car with scenic road access.

Can I do a day trip from Srinagar?

Yes, a day trip from Srinagar is possible, but we recommend an overnight stay to fully experience the Sonmarg landscape.

Is Sonmarg safe for tourists?

Yes, with premium local support and private travel arrangements, Sonmarg is safe and welcoming for tourists.

Can I combine Sonmarg with a Kashmir honeymoon package?

Absolutely. Sonmarg can be part of a honeymoon itinerary with romantic stays and private excursions.
