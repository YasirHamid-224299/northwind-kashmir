FAQs

Common questions about Gulmarg travel.

How long should I stay in Gulmarg?

A 1–2 night stay is common for Gulmarg highlights; extend if you plan multiple ski days or hikes.

Is Gulmarg suitable for beginners who want to ski?

Yes. We offer beginner-friendly lessons and guided slope time with certified instructors and appropriate equipment.

How do I reach Gulmarg?

Most visitors travel via Srinagar (by road) with private transfers arranged as part of the package.

Can I combine Gulmarg with other destinations?

Absolutely — we can include Gulmarg in a multi-destination Kashmir itinerary with Srinagar, Pahalgam, or Sonmarg.
