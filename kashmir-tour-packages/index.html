Best Time to Visit

Choose your Kashmir travel season with confidence.

Spring to Autumn

April through October is ideal for Kashmir tour packages. The weather is mild, gardens are blooming in Srinagar, and alpine meadows in Gulmarg and Sonmarg are accessible.

This season is perfect for honeymoon trips, luxury sightseeing, and exploring Pahalgam on foot.

Winter Travel

December to February brings snow to Gulmarg and Sonmarg. It is a beautiful time for luxury winter packages and cozy houseboat stays in Srinagar.

We recommend winter travelers prepare for colder nights and choose premium accommodations for comfort.
